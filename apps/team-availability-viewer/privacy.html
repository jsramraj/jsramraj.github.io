This Privacy Policy describes how Team Availability Viewer ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our extension.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information from Google Calendar™

The extension may access the following information needed to display team availability:

  • Calendar event data, including event titles, descriptions, dates, times, and attendees.
  • Lists of calendars you have access to.
  • Your email address and display name, where required for extension functionality.

1.2 Data Usage and Protection

We commit to:

  • Only accessing the data necessary for the extension's core functionality.
  • Never sharing or selling your data to third parties.
  • Using your data solely to present team availability clearly and reduce calendar clutter.
  • Providing transparent information about permissions required by the extension.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect only to provide and improve Team Availability Viewer. Specifically, to:

  • Identify and display leave, out-of-office, and work-from-home availability statuses.
  • Render a compact team availability view so users can quickly understand coverage.
  • Support extension configuration and basic user experience improvements.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

Team Availability Viewer is designed to minimize data retention and use platform-provided infrastructure and permissions. We do not sell personal data or transfer user data for unrelated advertising purposes.

3.2 User Preferences

If preferences are stored, they are used only to support extension behavior and user-selected settings.

3.3 Security Measures

We apply reasonable safeguards and rely on platform security controls to protect data handled by the extension.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Team Availability Viewer does not share your personal information or calendar data with third parties except when required by law, legal process, or to protect rights and safety.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You can stop using the extension at any time by uninstalling it. You can also revoke permissions from your Google account settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Team Availability Viewer is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date.
