Google Chrome Extension

Team Availability Viewer

Google Chrome extension that loads team leave plans from a shared Google Calendar and keeps your view clean

Install from Chrome Web Store

Many teams use a shared Google Calendar to track leave, work-from-home (WFH), and out-of-office (OOO) events. It works well, but as teams grow, these events can quickly clutter the calendar and make it difficult to focus on your own schedule.

Team Availability Viewer solves this by presenting your team's availability in a clean, compact view. With a single click, you can quickly see:

  • Who is on leave
  • Who is working from home
  • Who is available

No more scrolling through dozens of all-day events just to find out who is away. If your organization uses shared Google Calendars for team availability, this Chrome extension helps you keep the same visibility while reducing calendar clutter.
