2. Description of Service

Team Availability Sync is a Google Workspace Add-on designed to automatically synchronize Out-of-Office events from your personal calendar to selected team calendars. The Add-on identifies events with keywords such as "OOO," "Out of Office," "Vacation," or "Leave" in the title or description and creates corresponding events in team calendars you select.
